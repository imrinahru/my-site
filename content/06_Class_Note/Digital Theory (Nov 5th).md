## Lecture & Discussion Notes

### -human

consciousness/embodiment
Are we fully and only human?
The concept of human is malleable.
Haraway: Nested in the layers, senses are entangled with machines, other animals

- non-human
- more-than-human: Acknowledging the presence of other species and other intelligence in what makes us human, indigenous cosmology, Zoe Todd, a decade-ago, maybe outdated? Natasha Myers, how we are sensors, how we are connected with other non-human sentient beings?
- post-human: decentering the human, entangled with other systems (bio, machine) and technologically mediated, we didn't become post-human, but we've always been "post-human", although the term exploded with the advent of computer, cybernetics
- cyborg-human: a hybrid of machine and organism
- in-human: uncanny moment of subversion, a machine exceeds human perception and recognizability, with AI, sora1, glitches become the aesthetic, sora2, not-recognizable any more
